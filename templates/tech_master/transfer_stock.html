How to transfer stock:
  1. 1
    Select Source Branch

    Choose the branch that currently has the stock

  2. 2
    Select Destination Branch

    Choose the branch where the stock should go

  3. 3
    Select Product

    Choose the product you want to transfer

  4. 4
    Enter Quantity

    Specify how many units to transfer

  5. 5
    Submit Transfer

    Click the transfer button to complete

Note:
  • For single items, each unit is transferred individually
  • For bulk items, the quantity is moved as a batch
  • Transfers cannot be undone once completed
